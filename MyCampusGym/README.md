A mobile application to connect members of the campus community and facilitate gym use.

### Instalation Instructions

To install a Beta Version of MyCampusGym, visit https://testflight.apple.com/join/6AzyQQWs

#### Installation Requirements

To install MyCampusGym you must have an device running iOS 18.0 or later.


#### New User Setup

1. Select 'Don't have an account? Sign Up' from the bottom of the login page.
2. Enter the information and select 'Sign Up'
3. Check your email for a verification link (may take up to 10 minutes) - be sure to check your spam.
4. Login to MyCampusGym with your email address and password from Step 2.
5. Navigate to the side menu (icon in top left).
6. Select "Profile"
7. Select your name.
8. Select the "Campus" field to identify the campus you belong to.

### Feature Guide



#### MyFitness

To select one of the functions within the MyFitness feautre, tap the respective icon from the MyFitness tab.

- Occupancy: See the current open/closed status of facilities at your campus

- Classes: See a list of classes available at your campus.

#### MyBuddies

To view other users at your campus who are looking for a workout partner, select the MyBuddies tab.

- To connect with another user, swipe their profile to the right side of the screen.
- To see the next user instead, swipe their profile to the left.

#### MyMentor

My mentor will provide you with the ability to connect with a trainer or health professional to help meet your training goals. At this time, this feature is not yet available.

#### MyFeed

To see a list of FeedPosts from your campus, select the MyFeed tab.

- To read the full content of a post, tap the post.
- To comment on a post, tab the Comment box at the bottom of a post, enter your comment, and then select the post.
- To make a new post, tap the compose button in the top right.

#### MyJournal

To record your workouts, select the side-menu in the top left.

1. Tap "My Journal"
2. Select "Add New Workout"
3. Set the Start Time, End Time, and fil out the Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner as desired.
4. Select "Add Exercise" to add an exercise you wish to record.
5. Enter the Name of the Exercise, Sets, Reps, and Weight in pounds.
6. Tap "Add Exercise"
7. Repeat 4 thru 6 as needed.
8. Select "Save Workout"

#### MyPlan

To record your workout goals, select the side-menu in the top left and then tap "My Plan".

#### My Rewind

My rewind will provide an overview of your analystics across your previous workouts and recorded health data.

#### My Health

To record your health data, select the side-menu in the top left.

1. Tap "My Health"
2. Enter your Weidht, Height, and Height
3. Tap "Update Health Data"

#### Profile & Settings

To update your name, email, or campus, select the side-menu in the top left and then tap "Profile & Settings".

1. Select your profile icon/name section.
2. To edit your first name, last name, or campus select the corresponding field.
3. Update the respective data on the new screen.
4. Tap "Save"
